        Welcome to Tunnex
        
          
        Your data, your machine — before we begin.

              🔐
              End-to-end encrypted — Every dump is AES-256-GCM encrypted on your disk. Only you hold the encryption key.

        • 
              🖥️
              Stored locally only — Backups live on your machine. Nothing is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party — ever.
            
          • 
            
        • 
              🔑
              Secure SSH communication — Remote servers are reached exclusively over SSH with private-key authentication. Passphrases are held in memory only and never written to disk.
            
          • 
            
        • 
              🚫
              Zero telemetry — No analytics, no crash reports, no auto-updates, no phone-home. The app has no network access except to your own servers.
